Innovative Designs Inspired by nature

We specialize in rustic designs meant to reveal the natural characteristics of the materials we are working with. Our most popular projects are live-edge tables and furniture made from a variety of different hardwoods including black walnut and olive.

Our Promise To You

We will always deliver the highest quality designs and builds, made with true, North, strong and free Canadian wood. Our love of woodworking can be seen through the caliber of our products to ensure that you will always be reminded of the beauty of nature. From three brothers, with love.
